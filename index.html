Connect 4

Rules

Welcome to Connect 4!

Multiplayer:

This is a two-player game. You and your friend will take turns placing either red or yellow discs into the board. The pieces are dropped from the top and will drop down to the lowest available spot. When you get four of the same color pieces in lined up either vertically, horizontally, or diagonally, you will win the game. To place your piece, hover your mouse over the top of the board and click where you want to drop it.

Single-Player:

You will play against the computer, taking turns placing pieces. The first to get four in a row wins!
